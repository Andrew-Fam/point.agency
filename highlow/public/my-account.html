COMMON TRADING QUESTIONS

These are some of our most commonly asked questions. For a full FAQ click here.

How do I fund my account?

Log in to 'My Account' and click on 'Deposit' to fund your account. Deposits received by 2pm on weekdays will be processed by the close of that day. You will be notified by email once your account has been successfully funded. Please note that the minimum deposit amount is AUD $10.

How do I withdraw from my account?

Log in to 'My Account' and click on 'Withdrawal' and submit the amount you would like to withdraw. All withdrawal requests made by 2pm business days will be processed on the same day. Please allow between 1-3 working days for receipt of funds into your nominated bank account. The minimum amount of withdrawal is AUD $10.

Is there an upper limit for the total investment amount of multiple transactions?

For options that have not yet been settled prior to expiry time. The maximum investment amount for options which may be held (by our customers) is $100,000 AUD or USD.

Can I trade options just before the expiry time?

No. Please note that you can neither purchase nor close after the deadline/cutoff where trading is ceased but the option has not yet expired.
