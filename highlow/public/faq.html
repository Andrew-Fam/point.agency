What is a Spread HIGHLOW option?

A new type of option that shows a bid/ask spread, the standard method of price making in the forex markets.

Spread HIGHLOW offers payouts of 200%, or double your investment.

What are the 'underlying' assets?

The underlying assets are assets from which HIGHLOW Binary Options are derived.

Why does the spread narrow as the option moves closer to expiry?

Because the implied volatility is lower as the option matures, the spread narrows due the payout being fixed.

Is the spread fixed?

No, the spread is variable. In general, the shorter the time left until expiry, the narrower the spread.

Will rates be the same for options on the same instrument?

No, they do not always coincide. Although rates are delivered based on market conditions, HIGHLOW adopts a system where rate information is individually delivered for each option. As a result, rates may not coincide due to time lags in updates or other causes. With HIGHLOW, even if there are simultaneous options on the same instrument, payout rates are different and set individually; that is, we treat them as separate options.

Are individual options on the same instrument guaranteed to have the same expiry rate?

No, they are not guaranteed to be the same. HIGHLOW, adopts a system where rate information is individually delivered for each option. Even if there are options on the same instrument, payout rates are set individually; that is, we treat them as separate options.

What is a stale rate?

When processing a trade there are times when a delay occurs, i.e. the rate shown being different to the actual rate at the time, hence the stale rate error message. After a few seconds the new rate will refresh and the current rate will be once again available.
