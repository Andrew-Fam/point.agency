WHAT ARE BINARY OPTIONS?

As their name suggests, binary options are investment options that have two possible outcomes. Either your investment prediction is correct, or it is not. With the potential return known when the investment is made, binary options are also known as Fixed Return Options (FRO) or Digital Options (DO).

WHY BINARY OPTIONS?

For experienced FOREX traders and new investors alike, Binary Options continue to explode in popularity. Apart from the ease and convenience of trading Binary Options through HIGHLOW, Binary Options offer certainty with every trade. You can rest assured that there will always and only be one of three possible outcomes for your investment: that the option will expire in-the-money (win), out-of-the-money (lose), or at-the-money (even).

Unlike many other market-linked financial products, Binary Options offer greater ease and convenience. With shorter investment time frames, there is no need to constantly monitor your position, or be concerned with limit orders, stop orders or the dreaded margin call.

And while many investments have prohibitive 'costs of entry', you can be part of the exciting world of Binary Options trading through HIGHLOW with as little as AUD $10.
